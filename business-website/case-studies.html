NT
NovaTech ME FZCO
Technology · SaaS Platform
Market Entry & Free Zone Formation
The Challenge

A UK-based technology company wanted to enter the UAE market with no local knowledge, no network, and no understanding of which free zone suited their B2B SaaS model. They had a 60-day window to establish a legal entity before a key enterprise client contract expired.

Our Solution

Pinnacle conducted a market feasibility assessment and produced a detailed free zone comparison (DIFC vs. IFZA vs. DMCC). We recommended IFZA for its cost efficiency and activity flexibility, then managed the formation, visa processing for two founders, and banking introduction — all within the 60-day window.

AM
Al Mazrouei Group
Construction · Real Estate Development
Financial & Tax Advisory
The Challenge

A construction group with six subsidiary companies had inconsistent VAT record-keeping across all entities following rapid expansion. An FTA audit letter arrived without warning. The group faced potential penalties of over AED 200,000 and needed both an immediate audit response and a long-term compliance structure.

Our Solution

Our financial advisory team conducted a group-wide VAT reconciliation across all six entities, identified recoverable input VAT that had been missed, and managed the FTA audit response directly. We then implemented a unified bookkeeping system and took over monthly VAT filings for the entire group.

GT
Gulf Tech Solutions
IT Services · Infrastructure
PRO & Visa Services
The Challenge

An IT services company had won a major government contract requiring 23 new employees to be onboarded within six weeks. The scope included engineers from six different nationalities, each with different attestation and qualification requirements. Existing in-house PRO capacity could not handle the volume.

Our Solution

We dedicated a three-person PRO team exclusively to this project, processing all 23 employment visa files in parallel. We handled attestation in four countries simultaneously through our network of legalisation agents, managed MOHRE quota applications, and scheduled biometrics in batches to avoid appointment delays.
