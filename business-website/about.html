Our Story

From frustration to the UAE’s most trusted formation consultancy

Pinnacle Corporate Services was founded in 2011 by a team of UAE business formation specialists who had seen one too many founders lose months — and sometimes everything — to avoidable compliance errors and bureaucratic delays.

We set out to build something different: a consultancy where the answer was always honest, the timeline always realistic, and the process always managed end-to-end. No runaround. No hidden fees. No surprises.

Today we operate from Business Bay, Dubai with a team of 18 specialists covering company formation, visa services, financial advisory, and market entry. We have formed over 200 companies across 30+ industries, with a 98% client satisfaction rate and zero compliance failures on our watch.

Our Journey

Built Milestone by Milestone

From a two-person consultancy in 2011 to the UAE’s go-to formation partner for international founders.

2011

Founded in Business Bay, Dubai

Pinnacle launched with a two-person team and a simple promise: honest advice, transparent fees, and end-to-end management. First client: a UK tech entrepreneur setting up a DIFC entity.

2014

PRO & Visa Practice Launched

Responding to client demand, we expanded to offer dedicated PRO services, document attestation, and the full visa lifecycle — adding 6 PRO specialists to the team.

2017

DIFC-Licensed Financial Advisory Division

Obtained our DIFC Category 3 licence to provide regulated financial advisory services. Onboarded our first CFO-on-demand client, a PE-backed retail group with 12 UAE entities.

2020

100 Active Clients Across 30 Industries

Reached a milestone of 100 active client relationships. Expanded to serve the healthcare, technology, and logistics sectors. Launched our market entry consulting practice for international expansion mandates.

2024

200+ Formations — Zero Compliance Failures

Surpassed 200 company formations with a perfect compliance record. Expanded the team to 18 specialists and launched our HR & Talent Solutions practice to support workforce growth for our clients.

The Values That Drive Us

Four principles that have not changed since 2011 — and never will.

Honesty First
We tell you what is possible, not what you want to hear. If a business activity will not get government approval, we say so upfront — before you spend a dirham. Our reputation is built on clients trusting our word.
Speed with Precision
Fast does not mean careless. We hold a 9-day average formation time because we have invested in the processes, relationships, and checklists that eliminate errors — not by cutting corners.
Full Accountability
One team, one relationship manager, end-to-end ownership. When you work with Pinnacle, you have a single point of contact who takes full responsibility — not a call centre that passes you around.
Local Depth
